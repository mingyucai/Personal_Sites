Bio

In February 2021, I joined the Air Lab in Lehigh University supervised by Prof. Cristian-Ioan Vasile .

In December 2020, I completed my dissertation of Doctoral degree.

In Auguest 2020, I received Graduate College Post-comprehensive Research Fellowship.

In February 2020, I completed my comprehensive exam and became a PhD candidate in Uiowa.

In December 2019, I received Graduate & Professional Student Government (GSPG) Grants.

In October 2019, I received Graduate College Presentation Travel/Virtual Conference Funds.

In August 2017, I joined the Networked Systems and Robotics (NSR) supervised by Prof. Zhen Kan and Prof. Shaoping Xiao at the Mechanical Engieering in University of Iowa (UIOWA) as a PhD student. My research mainly relates to motion planning employing formal methods and mechaine learning techniques.

In June 2015, I pursued the M.Sc. degree within the programme Aerospace Engineering from University of Florida.

In August 2011, I became a Bachelor student in Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT).

I was born in at Kunming, China.
